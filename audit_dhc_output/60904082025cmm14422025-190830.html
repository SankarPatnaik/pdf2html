Simultaneously, for causing delay in the matter and for not mentioning the aforesaid documents in the affidavit at the earliest available opportunity, defendant no. 2 (petitioner herein) is burdened with cost of Rs. 15,000/which shall be paid to the plaintiff on the next date of hearing before the learned Trial Court, which is stated to be of tomorrow i.e. 05.08.2025.
